Privacy Policy - Dutch Flashcards

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Dutch Flashcards is an offline language-learning application for Android.

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network communication. Your learning progress (current card position) is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

The app requests no permissions. Speech is generated by your device's built-in text-to-speech engine.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.
